About Milky Way

The Milky Way Movie Theatre first opened its doors on January 15, 1994 and was founded and based in Indiana. The Milky Way movie theatre is based off an outer space experience while getting ready to watch your favorite movie in one of our 8 unique screens. Here is a quote from the owner, Carlos Bustos, about the movie theatre:

Do not wonder what it would feel like to experience space, the experience is here in your own neighborhood.
